Smart Cane for the Visually Impaired

Development of an intelligent cane designed to assist visually impaired individuals. The system integrates a GPS module for real-time location tracking and ultrasonic sensors to detect nearby obstacles, enhancing navigation safety and autonomy.

Technologies used: ESP32 microcontroller, GPS module, Ultrasonic sensors.
